# Final Project Conclusion

* **End-to-End System:** Engineered an automated AI engine for proactive next-month sentiment forecasting.
* **Data Foundation:** Processed **4,021 customer reviews** across the iPhone 15, iPhone 16, and iQOO Z10.
* **Data Standardization:** Ensured quality through rigorous text cleaning and automated PII removal.
* **Strategic Augmentation:** Balanced data distributions to prevent bias toward overwhelmingly positive reviews.
* **Diverse NLP Evaluation:** Assessed structural (**BERT**), lexical (**VADER**), and keyword (**Word Cloud**) NLP models.
* **Ensemble Innovation:** Used **Soft Voting** and **Bagging** to fuse scores and eliminate specific weaknesses.
* **Accuracy Milestones:** Validated standalone deep learning models yielding highly accurate results (**~87% accuracy**).
* **Optimal Ensemble Metrics:** Showed **Soft Voting** maximizes reliability (F1: **0.542**) while **Bagging** minimizes error (RMSE: **0.897**).
* **Future Forecasting:** Mapped internal scores to standard 1-5 star ratings to predict market behavior.
* **Actionable Predictions:** Forecasted the iPhone 16 to maintain a stable **4.467 star rating** (**91.5% positive**).
* **Final Delivery:** Culminated the full AI predictive pipeline into an accessible **Interactive Dashboard**.
* **Business Impact:** Empowered stakeholders with a visual tool for data-driven, proactive market strategies.
