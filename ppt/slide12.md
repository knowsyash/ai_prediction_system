# Interactive Business Dashboard

## Dashboard Overview
The final component of our architecture is an interactive analytics interface (`create_dashboard.py`), seamlessly connected to our entire data pipeline. It serves as the visual front-end for business users to interact with the findings.

## Key Features & Insights
* **Trend Visualization:** Offers live tracking of product rating forecasts alongside historical scraped metrics.
* **Sentiment Breakdown:** Granular, visual breakdowns of Positive vs. Negative review volumes filtered by specific smartphone models (iPhone 15, iPhone 16, iQOO Z10).
* **Actionable ROI Intelligence:** Replaces raw datasets with readable insights, allowing product teams to immediately see which devices are trending downward.
* **Pipeline Integration:** This dashboard proves the system's end-to-end viability—taking raw e-commerce product pages and directly transforming them into predictive corporate intelligence.
