# Problem Statement

## Core Challenges
* **Data Overload:** Thousands of unstructured reviews are impossible to analyze manually.
* **Missed Feedback:** Critical hardware/software issues often go unnoticed.
* **Blind Spots:** Brands struggle to project how current sentiment impacts future ratings.
* **No Prioritization:** Lack of a data-driven way to prioritize product improvements.

## Why It Matters
* **Consumer Trust:** Most buyers rely heavily on online ratings for expensive tech purchases.
* **Revenue Risk:** Undetected negative trends directly lead to sales drops.
* **Proactive Action:** Predicting ratings helps brands fix bugs faster and adapt strategies.
